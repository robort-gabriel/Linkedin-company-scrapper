How to Use
  1. Navigate to LinkedIn and search for companies (e.g., "digital marketing agencies")
  2. Make sure you're on the search results page
  3. Optional: Import previous data (JSON/CSV) - imported companies will be skipped during scraping
  4. Set the maximum number of pages to process (default: 5)
  5. Click "Start Collection" to begin capturing data from pages you're viewing
  6. Watch as data is collected in real-time (companies already in your list are automatically skipped)
  7. Export your data as JSON or CSV when complete
  8. Delete individual companies using the delete button in each row
  9. Duplicate companies are automatically skipped (both imported and newly scraped)

Important: This extension helps you capture information from LinkedIn pages you are actively viewing. It operates on user-visible content and respects LinkedIn's terms of service. Use responsibly and in accordance with LinkedIn's usage policies.
